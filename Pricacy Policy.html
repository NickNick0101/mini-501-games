Privacy Policy for Mini Games - All in one

Last updated: February 13, 2023

Introduction:

Thank you for using Mini Games - All in one for Android, which is owned and operated by Phlox app infotech. We understand that privacy is important to you, and we are committed to protecting the personal information that you share with us. This Privacy Policy describes the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and with whom we share it.

Types of information we collect:

We do not collect any personal information from our users. The only information we collect is the standard data that is automatically collected by the Google Play Store, such as the number of installs, user ratings, and reviews.

Use of information:

The information we collect is used solely to improve the performance and user experience of Mini Games - All in one. We do not share this information with any third-party companies or organizations.

Third-party services:

Mini Games - All in one uses the following third-party services:

  • Chartboost for monetization purposes. Chartboost collects non-personal information such as IP address, device type, and operating system version to show relevant ads to our users and to improve the performance of the service. For more information on Chartboost's privacy policy, please visit their website at https://www.chartboost.com/privacy/.
  • Firebase Analytics to help us understand how our users interact with the app and to improve our services. Firebase Analytics collects information such as app usage, device information, and user demographics. For more information on Firebase Analytics' privacy policy, please visit their website at https://firebase.google.com/policies/analytics.

Changes to our Privacy Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices and services. If we make any material changes, we will provide notice to you through the Google Play Store or by other means, such as email.

Contact us:

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at phloxappinfotech@gmail.com.
